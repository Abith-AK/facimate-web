FACIMATE

An interactive application for managing gated communities, including member and visitor details, vehicle entries, complaints/services, amenities booking, events, announcements, meter readings, and bill payments.


Facimate provides a complete solution that ensures convenience for residents and community management, thereby enhancing the community experience. It automates and simplifies the operations of any society, regardless of size, brings stability to society processes, and offers residents access to all the information they need.
