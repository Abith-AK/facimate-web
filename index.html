Booking

Facimate simplifies communication between residents and the main gate security by keeping track of visitors entering and exiting the community. It notifies residents about their visitors entry and exit, and allows them to pre-register visitors to reduce waiting times at the gate.

Facimate is a system that records the details of vehicles entering and exiting a community. The system provides an up-to-date list of all the vehicles that pass through the gate, along with their check-in and check-out times. The administrative staff can use the search option to view vehicle details for a specific vehicle number.

Residents can raise any complaints or service requests, and the admin staff will review each request and assign tasks to the corresponding technician or support staff to fix the issue. Both the admin staff and residents receive real-time updates on the service status, which helps to reduce escalations.

Facimate's poll feature allows residents to vote any time and from anywhere, promoting community participation and timely notifications.

Facimate provides residents with a user-friendly platform to manage invoices, payments and receipts with ease. Notifications are sent to residents to alert them of their dues and payment status.

Facimate provides residents with direct access to book available amenities and ensure transparency in the booking process, making all amenities equally available to everyone. This means the residents can now enjoy community amenities without any wait time.
